Our Trips

Beginner Bliss: Scenic Family Float

Duration: 2-3 hours
Difficulty Level: Easy (Class I-II rapids)

Perfect for families and first-timers, this trip offers a gentle introduction to rafting. Glide through calm, scenic stretches of the river, surrounded by breathtaking views of lush forests and wildlife. Along the way, enjoy a few mild rapids that add just the right touch of excitement. Our friendly guides will share fascinating stories about the area and ensure a safe, memorable experience for all ages.

Adventure Seeker: Classic Whitewater Thrill

Duration: Half-day (4-5 hours)
Difficulty Level: Moderate (Class III-IV rapids)

This is the ultimate adventure for thrill-seekers! Conquer challenging rapids like "The Rollercoaster" and "Big Drop" as you navigate twisting channels and roaring waves. With expert guides leading the way, you’ll feel the adrenaline rush while mastering teamwork to tackle each rapid. Between the action-packed stretches, enjoy stunning vistas of canyon walls and peaceful pools perfect for catching your breath.

Extreme Rush: The Ultimate Whitewater Expedition

Duration: Full-day (6-8 hours) or Overnight
Difficulty Level: Advanced (Class IV-V rapids)

Take on the wildest rapids and push your limits with this high-intensity rafting experience. Designed for experienced rafters and adventure junkies, this trip takes you deep into rugged terrain, with technical rapids like "The Gauntlet" and "Devil’s Staircase." Expect heart-pounding drops, swirling eddies, and a true wilderness adventure. Overnight trips include riverside camping under the stars with gourmet campfire meals.
